Emaar SCO Plots Gurgaon

SCO (Shop Cum Office) is a concept that infuses retail and commercial usage plots with freedom to offer customisations, with all basic hygiene elements of a marketplace, in a well-integrated ecosystem. Marketplaces such as Connaught place Delhi, Sec 29 Gurugram and Sector 17 in Chandigarh are some examples of the concept of SCO Plots.

Emaar SCO Plots could propel your enterprise towards kick-starting your start-up idea, or towards fulfilling your dream of opening a restaurant or utilising the space for office operations. The concept of SCO is a richly gifted ecosystem supporting retail or office in a flourishing marketplace is fast finding currency amongst investors. With wide frontage, ample parking space, adequate power backups and other common facilities such as a provision to build multiple floors, Plots for Shop-Cum-Office offer immense possibilities for investors and business owners.
